Rules of the Game

  • Log-in by entering your username and password.
  • Answer the first question
  • The correct answer will be revealed.
  • Move on to the next location
  • Answer the question
  • For each right question you get 10 points
  • For each skipped question 5 points are subtracted
  • For each wrong question 3 points are subtracted
